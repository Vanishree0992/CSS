Creamy Garlic Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add heavy cream, bring to a simmer, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add cooked pasta and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve warm.
